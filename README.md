# makmur-masjid-os
Project Makmur is designed to help Mosque Committees (AJK) efficiently manage crowds, resources, and volunteers during the busy month of Ramadan. It eliminates chaotic physical queues, reduces food waste, and digitizes donations.

## 👥 Contributors
We are proud to acknowledge the talented developers who have built and maintained Project Makmur. Their dedication ensures that mosques can serve their communities more effectively.
